Coral Restoration Initiatives

Join us in preserving our oceans! We offer hands-on programs for coral restoration, including reef planting, monitoring, and education. Contact us today to get involved in our free introductory session!

We provide:

  • Expert Guides and Scientists
  • No Long-Term Commitments
  • Flexible Participation Schedules
  • Seasonal Dive Events
  • Educational Workshops

Reef Planting

We offer reef planting sessions for all ages, starting from age 10 with adult supervision. Whether you're a beginner or experienced diver, we have programs to suit your skills. Learn techniques for transplanting coral fragments, understanding marine ecosystems, and contributing to biodiversity. Participating helps develop environmental awareness and teamwork.

Coral Monitoring

We teach coral monitoring for beginners, intermediate, and advanced participants. Improve your skills with weekly sessions. Learn new tips and techniques from marine biologists. We also have citizen science programs where you can track reef health and contribute data to global efforts!

Educational Programs

We offer educational programs for all ages, beginning at age 6. Our sessions cover a variety of topics, from coral bleaching to sustainable fishing. Our experts will help you understand techniques and achieve your conservation goals. We work with you to create a customized program, tailored to your interests.
