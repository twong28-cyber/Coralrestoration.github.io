Welcome to Coral Restoration. Our mission is to restore, protect, and sustain coral reefs in their natural environment. We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to rehabilitating damaged coral ecosystems.

Our team provides ongoing care for coral reefs in need. We also support long-term conservation efforts for reefs that require sustained protection.

Will you partner with us? We need dedicated volunteers to help with restoration projects. You can also donate, become a member, or become a sponsor. Contact us today.
